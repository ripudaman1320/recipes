RECIPE FOR MAKING MAGGY

Ingredients:
1.A pack of maggie
2.A glass water
3.Vegetables you like

Procedure

Take a pan and add a glass of water into it. Now add maggy.Add maggie masala into it and stir the contents for about 30 sec.Add vegetables as per your requirement. Now leave it on low temperature on gas for about 3-4 min. Serve it in a plate with  huge smile.
